Professional Goals

Where I'm headed

My career has been a series of deliberate steps toward the same place: work that lets me lead, communicate, and build. Here's how I see the next chapters.

Near-Term · 1–2 Years

Step into a software engineering role

My immediate goal is to join a team as a software engineer, where I can apply my full-stack and AI skills to real products while learning from experienced engineers. I want to ship code that people rely on, contribute to a codebase larger than my own, and bring the discipline and reliability I've built in operations to an engineering environment.

What I'm working toward

  • Contribute to a production codebase on a collaborative team
  • Deepen my skills in full-stack development and applied AI
  • Grow through code review, mentorship, and shared ownership
  • Use my clearance and communication strengths to add value fast

The direction I'm growing

  • Lead engineering efforts and mentor other builders
  • Bridge technical teams and the people they serve
  • Turn complex problems into clear, shippable solutions
  • Build technology that supports important missions
Long-Term · 5+ Years

Grow into technical leadership

Over time, I want to grow into a role that sits where I've always been most effective: between the technology and the people. That might look like technical leadership, where I help a team build the right thing and communicate its value clearly to everyone who depends on it. My background in organizational communication and military leadership is a foundation for exactly that kind of work.

What Guides Me

The through-line in every goal

Keep building

I learn best by making something real. Whatever the title, I want to stay close to the work of building useful things.

Keep communicating

Technology only matters when people can understand and use it. I'll keep making complex things clear.

Keep serving

A decade in uniform taught me to work for something bigger than myself. I want my work to matter.

Building toward these goals now

If your team is looking for someone who leads, communicates, and builds, I'd love to talk.
